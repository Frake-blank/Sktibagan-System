About SK Tibagan

Serving the youth. Building the community. Promoting transparency.

Our Mission

To empower the youth of Barangay Tibagan through inclusive programs, transparency, and community-driven initiatives.

👁️

Our Vision

A united, active, and responsible youth community that contributes to sustainable local development.

🤝

Our Values

Transparency, leadership, service, accountability, and youth participation in governance.

Who We Are

The Sangguniang Kabataan of Barangay Tibagan is committed to representing the youth and ensuring their voices are heard. Through projects, profiling, and engagement, we aim to create meaningful impact in the community.

This website serves as a platform for transparency, updates, and communication between the SK Council and the residents of Barangay Tibagan.
